Ambassador Azad Dhomun pays a courtesy visit to Adv. Anton Salman, Mayor of Bethlehem

Uncategorized
December 6, 2019December 9, 2019 Karen Mancini0

During his courtesy visit to the Municipality of Bethlehem, Amb. Azad Dhomun, one of the founders of the relationship between Mauritius and Palestine, meets Adv. The Mayor of Bethlehem, Anton Salman. 

Ambassador Dhomun was presented by the Mayor with an insight into the situation in Bethlehem and Palestine. Mayor Salman addressed the value of a strong relationship with Mauritius, whose population celebrates the richness of diversity like the Palestinians. 

Mayor Salman welcomed Mauritius ‘ wish to enhance coordination with Bethlehem and highlighted the potential for collaborative efforts between Bethlehem and Quatre Bornes. Adv. Anton Salman also emphasized the need for Mauritius to continue its promotion of liberty and justice for the Palestinians at the African level and in international forums.

Amb. Dhomun conveyed the unconditional support of Mauritius for Palestine and the desire to promote and establish ties with Bethlehem that began when Xavier Luc Duval and Dhomun visited Bethlehem four years ago, the then Deputy Prime Minister and today’s leader of the opposition in Mauritius.
